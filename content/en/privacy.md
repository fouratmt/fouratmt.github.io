This policy explains how [fourat.dev](https://fourat.dev/) handles information when you visit the website or contact me.

## Website hosting and delivery

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages and delivered through Cloudflare. These infrastructure providers process technical request data needed to operate, secure, and troubleshoot their services. This may include your IP address, browser and device information, requested URL, request time, and security or network-error information.

Their handling of this data is governed by their respective privacy policies:

- [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
- [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

## Analytics and cookies

This website does not currently load Google Analytics, advertising pixels, or other client-side analytics trackers. It does not set an analytics or advertising cookie.

The light/dark theme preference is saved in your browser's `localStorage`. It remains on your device and is used only to restore your chosen appearance on later visits. You can remove it by clearing this site's browser storage.

## Email contact

If you use an email link, your email application sends the information you choose to provide. I use it only to reply to your message and manage any resulting professional conversation. Please do not send sensitive personal data that is unnecessary for your request.

## External links

The website links to third-party sites such as LinkedIn and client or employer websites. Those sites apply their own privacy policies when you visit them.

## Your questions and rights

For a privacy question or a request concerning information you have sent directly to me, email [mastouri.fourat@gmail.com](mailto:mastouri.fourat@gmail.com). Rights and retention obligations may depend on the applicable law and the context of our exchange.
